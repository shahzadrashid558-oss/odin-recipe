Greek Salad

Description

A fresh and healthy Mediterrane salad with crisp vegetables and feta cheese.

Ingredients

  • Chopped cucumbers
  • Cherry tomatoes
  • Feta cheese cubes
  • Olive oil and vinegar

Steps

  1. Wash and chop all the vegetables.
  2. Place vegetables in a large bowl.
  3. Top with feta cheese and dressing.
  4. Toss gently and serve chilled.
