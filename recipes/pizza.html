Pizza

Description

A quick and crispy homemade pepperoni Pizza.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough
  • Pizza sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni

Steps

  1. Perheat oven to 475°F (275°C).
  2. Spread sauce and cheese on dough.
  3. Add pepperoni toppings.
  4. Bake for 12 minutes until golden.
